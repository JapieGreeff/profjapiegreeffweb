Affiliations

These are the formal ties I have with various institutions and bodies. Click on any one to get more details.

Council Member of the Big Data and Artificial Intelligence Professional Working Committee of the International Alliance of Skills Development

I have been a member of this international body since 2023 as a council member, and since then have presented at the annual meeting in China in 2023 and 2024. This body formed during the creation of the BRICS Machine Learning and Big Data Standards where I was a part of the drafting committee.

Expert Participant in the Working Group Drafting the BRICS Group Standard for Data Analysis and Visualisation Curriculum Section

I was elected as an expert to assist in the drafting of the BRICS Standard on Data Analysis and Visualisation in the Curriculum Section of the standard in 2024. This standard has since been finalised in 2025.

Associate of the National Institute of Theoretical and Computational Science (NITheCS)

I have been an associate of the National Institute of Theoretical and Computational Science since 2021, where I have participated in the work of the institute by presenting a number of colloqiums, Mini-Schools, and a SATACS course on Paralell Computing and Artificial Intelligence.

Extraordinary Professor at the Optentia Research Unit at the North-West University

In 2025 I was appointed an extraordinary professor at the North-West University in the Optentia Research Unit where I have presented a number of training sessions on using Artificial Intelligence, supervised students in multidisciplinary projects and worked with researchers in the unit on large projects.

Member of the SABS/TC0001/SC42 technical Committee on Artificial Intelligence

I am a member of the committee that oversees the acceptance of international standards into the South African context, specifically working with Artificial Intelligence standards.
