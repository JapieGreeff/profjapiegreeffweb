Accomplishments

During my career I have won a couple of awards. Below are links to the individual awards themselves.

SAPHE Research Excellence Award 2025 - First Runner Up

This award was granted for the work done in 2025 relating to computer science education, from theoretical roots to pedagogical and curriculum research.

BRICS Excellence Expert Coaching Award 2023 and 2024

I was awarded the Excellent Expert award three times, twice during 2023 and 2024 for coaching students in Data Analysis and Visualisation, and once in 2023 for coaching students in Machine Learning and Big Data for the BRICS International Skills Challenges Finals held in China. My students won Silver and Bronze in 2023, and Bronze in 2024.

BRICS International Industrial Innovation Contest 2023 - Second Prize

The BRICS Innovation Contest takes place internationally every year, and in 2023 when I took part there were more than a thousand contestants that took part accross eight tracks. In my track I managed to take second place with a project titled “Opportunity Exploration with AI Assistance”.

NWU Faculty of Natural and Agricultural Science Community Engagement Award 2021

In 2021 I was involved in a number of community engagement projects in various informal settlements and rural communities, along with coordinating a number of exhibitions for the 2021 National Science Week for that year. For all the different contributions I was awarded the faculty community engagement award for the year.

South African Institute of Electrical Engineers Student Project Awards 2011 - First Prize

When I finished my Bachelors in Technology degree, I was elected to represent my university, Tshwane University of Technology, at the annual South African Institute of Electrical Engineers Student Project Awards Competition. My final year project was based on novel work I was doing in industry to create generic test fixtures that could be used during the manufacturing process to test different kinds of products by swapping out the front end of the fixture that comprises of the spring loaded connection pins with different front ends that were made from circuit boards as strength members and generic back end. This was work that I continued on with in my Masters degree where I was also focused on high volume manufacturing.
